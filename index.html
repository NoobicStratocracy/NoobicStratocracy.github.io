Frequently Asked Questions
What are the requirements to join?

Infantrymen simply must be at least 13 years old, have Discord and Roblox accounts, and demonstrate basic brain function.

What training is provided?

New recruits undergo basic training covering weapons, protocol, and group information. More training is given to those who join other divisions.

How do I rank up?

Promotions are handed out via recommendation from an NCO or Officer and are based on merit, time in group, activity, reputation, & overall reliability and ethic.
