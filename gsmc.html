Key Roles

Corpsman

Corpsmen are the backbone of the GSMC, they consist of most of its forces. Corpsmen are competent at rendering aid and stabilizing wounded until more advanced aid can be rendered.

Senior Corpsmen

Senior Corpsmen are experienced medics who have proven themselves competent on the field of battle. Senior Corpsmen are also active members of the medical community and assist their peers when needed.

Field Surgeon

Field Surgeons are the officers of the GSMC, able to host events and lead small squads of medics on the battlefield. Field Surgeons are experienced in leadership and all aspects of the Medical Corps.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the criteria to enlist?

Candidates must be an infantryman for 1 Week or more.

Is prior medical experience required?

No, we offer full training as you progress; however having some knowledge will assist you.

What if we don't want to roleplay?

The GSMC offers 2 platoons, the 29th platoon focuses on roleplay, while the 33rd platoon focuses on combat support, enlistees can choose either.
